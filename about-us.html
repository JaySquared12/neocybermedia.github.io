About Us

        Neo Cybermedia is an ePortfolio made by our group, Group 2. Our group is from section 12 STEM-Euclid in Eusebio Senior High School, Pasig City. In this ePortfolio, we can put our various works and outputs related to our subject Media and Information Literacy so we can utilize and use the technology we have right now. Media and Information Literacy are the essential skills and competencies that allow individuals to engage with media and other information providers effectively, as well as develop critical thinking and life-long learning skills to socialize and become active citizens.
